Cinnamon Pancakes

Delicious cinnamon pancakes with toppings of your choice.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Oil
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Cake flour
  1. Pour one egg into a mixing bowl.
  2. Add 50 ml of sugar into the bowl.
  3. Add one and a half tbsps of oil into the bowl.
  4. Add a pinch of salt.
  5. Add 150ml of milk into the bowl and mix until everything is blended.
  6. Add 268ml of flour into the bowl.
  7. Add a sprinkle of cinnamon.
  8. Add one tsp of baking podwer and mix until the batter is smoooth and consisitent.
  9. Spray non-stick spray onto the pan and fry over medium heat until the pancakes are golden brown on each side.
  10. Serve with syrup or honey, and banana toppings (or any topping of your choice).
