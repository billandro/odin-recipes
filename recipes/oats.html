Oats Breakie

Crunchy oats breakie

This is a simple, nutricious, and crunchy oats breakfast that will take you less than 10 minutes to make.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Red apple
  • Cinnamon
  • Walnuts
  • Plain Yoghurt
  • Honey/Syrup

Instructions:

  1. Pour four spoons of oats into a bowl.
  2. Add two and a half spoons of plain yoghurt into the bowl.
  3. Add a bit of cinnamon.
  4. Cut the red apple in half, grate the half and add it to the bowl.
  5. Stir the contents of the bowl until everything is meshed together.
  6. Heat up a pan on the stove on medium heat.
  7. Chop the other half of the apple into pieces of your own choice and add it to the pan.
  8. Add two spoonfulls of oats into the pan, followed by a sprinke of cinnamon.
  9. Wait until the flavour of the oats, apples and cinnamon are potent.
  10. Add the contents of the pan into the bowl.
  11. Add two and a half spoons of plain yoghurt into the bowl, and drizzle it with honey.
