Toasted Eggs

Delicious toasted eggs

This easy-to-make toasted egg snack will change your life.

Ingredients

  • Bread (The quantity of slices is entirely up to you).
  • Mayonnaise
  • Margarine
  • Eggs (The quantity is up to you).
  • Cheese
  • Oil
  1. Toast two slices of bread.
  2. Apply mayonnaise on the toasted bread.
  3. Sprinkle the cheese of your choice on top of the mayonnasie you just applied.
  4. In a pan pour in a bit oil and fry the eggs over medium heat.
  5. Once eggs are cooked to your liking, apply them onto the toasted bread.
  6. Your snack is now ready to be devoured ;).
